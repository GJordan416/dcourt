Federal Law Clerk Hiring Plan

Application Process

The U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia hires law clerks for one-year terms. Below is the procedure for applying.

  • Application deadline: March 1st of each year
  • Required documents: Resume, Cover Letter, Writing Sample, Law School Transcripts
  • Interview process: Candidates will be contacted for interviews after initial screening

For further details, refer to the Official Hiring Guidelines.
