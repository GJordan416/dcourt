Seminar Attendance Policy

Judges are required to disclose their attendance at privately funded seminars. The purpose of this policy is to maintain transparency and avoid conflicts of interest.

  • Judges must disclose any seminar they attend that is funded by a third party.
  • The disclosure must include the name of the event, the funding source, and the amount spent.

For full details on this policy, please refer to the Disclosure Guidelines.
