Naturalization Information

Learn more about the naturalization process and requirements for becoming a U.S. citizen.

The District Court handles all naturalization petitions filed in this jurisdiction. To begin the process, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the Application for Naturalization (Form N-400).
  2. Attend a biometrics appointment, if required.
  3. Schedule an interview with a USCIS officer.
  4. If approved, you will be scheduled for an oath ceremony.

For more details, visit the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services website or contact the Clerk's office for assistance.
