After Hours Filing

This section provides information on how to file documents with the court outside of regular operating hours.

For emergency filings after 4:00 p.m., please follow the procedures outlined below:

  • Submit your filing through the Electronic Case Filing (ECF) system.
  • If ECF submission is not possible, follow the guidelines for physical filing and contact the after-hours court officer.
  • Be sure to include a statement of urgency and your contact information with your filing.
