OSCAR - Online System for Clerkship Application & Review

About OSCAR

The Online System for Clerkship Application and Review (OSCAR) allows law students and recent graduates to apply for judicial clerkships electronically. Below are the key features of OSCAR:

  • Submit applications, resumes, and writing samples
  • Monitor application status
  • Communicate with judges and court staff

For access to the OSCAR system, visit OSCAR website.
